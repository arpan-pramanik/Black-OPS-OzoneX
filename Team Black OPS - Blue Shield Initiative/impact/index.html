impacts

impact: Sea Level Likely to Rise 1 to 6.6 Feet by 2100

The gradual increase in ocean height threatens coastal communities, leading to erosion and freshwater contamination.

impact: Hurricanes Will Become Stronger and More Intense

Warmer sea temperatures provide energy for hurricanes, leading to increased rainfall and flooding risks for coastal regions

impact:More Droughts and Heat Waves

Climate change enhances drought severity and frequency, affecting food security and water resources.

impact: Longer Wildfire Season

Warmer temperatures and prolonged dry spells contribute to longer wildfire seasons, threatening wildlife and human life.

impact:Global Temperatures Will Continue to Rise

Ongoing greenhouse gas emissions are expected to significantly raise global temperatures, affecting ecosystems and human health.

impact:Arctic Is Very Likely to Become Ice-Free

Global warming is leading to the loss of Arctic ice, which has severe implications for wildlife and global weather patterns.
